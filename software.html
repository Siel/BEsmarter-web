Bayesian Spatial Fixed Effects Panel Data Models

This is a free Graphical User Interface to estimate 8 different especifications of Fixed Effects Spatial Panel Data Models through a Bayesian approach. In addition, you can calculate the Posterior Matrix Probability of the contiguity matrices that you define.
For tha GUI we have, please add at the end of the explanation: See the paper "The inteplay between the Bayesian and frequentist approaches: a general nesting spatial panel data model" for details.

Software

Hunting Black Swans

This is a free Graphical User Interface to estimate the bet size associated with a speficif football match. It requieres historial records of the football match, and betting odds from different bookmakers of it. See the paper "Profiting from the English Premier League: Predictive Elicitation, the Kelly Criterion and Black Swans" for details.
